# Old Dirty Buffalo

## Ingredients

 * Bourbon - 1 1/4 oz
 * Campari - 1 1/4 oz
 * Orange Juice - 3/4 oz
 * Simple Syrup - 1/3 oz
 * Maraschino Cherry
 * Ice
 * Soda Water

## Directions

 * Muddle Maraschino Cherry in a Tom Collins glass
 * Combine Bouron, Campari, Orange Juice, and Simple Syrup
 * Stir until chilled
 * Pour into Tom Collins glass
 * Top with Ice and Soda Water
