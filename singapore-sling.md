# Singapore Sling

## Ingredients

 * Gin - 1 1/2 oz
 * Benedictine - 1 oz
 * Lime Juice - 1 oz
 * Simple Syrup - 1/4 oz
 * Maraschino Cherry Syrup - 1/4 oz
 * Ice
 * Soda Water
 * Maraschino Cherry

## Directions

 * Combine Gin, Benedictine, Lime Juice, Simple Syrup, and Maraschino Cherry Syrup
 * Shake with ice
 * Pour into Tom Collins glass
 * Top with Ice and Soda Water
 * Garnish with Maraschino Cherry
